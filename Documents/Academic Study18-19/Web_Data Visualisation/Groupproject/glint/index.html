Background

This report presents statistics on the demographic and socio-economic characteristics of the population in mid-2017 at the District Council (DC) district level. These statistics are primarily computed based on data collected in the General Household Survey (GHS) in January to December 2017 and the 2017 mid-year population estimates by age and sex in each DC district as control totals.
